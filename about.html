Our Story

Managed IT Done Right Since 2008

AGS was founded in Troy, Michigan with a clear mission: provide businesses with the kind of enterprise-grade IT support and security that was once only available to large corporations.

What started as a small team of dedicated IT professionals has grown into a full-service managed IT and compliance organization. We've built our reputation on being proactive rather than reactive — solving problems before they become crises and keeping our clients ahead of evolving threats.

Today, AGS is one of the region's leading specialists in CMMC compliance for Department of Defense contractors, alongside a comprehensive suite of managed IT, cybersecurity, and cloud services.

  • Founded in Troy, MI — serving businesses locally and across the country
  • CMMC Level 2 compliance specialists for DoD contractors
  • Certified team with deep expertise across security frameworks
  • Customer-centric approach: your goals drive our strategy

What We Know

Our Areas of Expertise
